Ambassador Kate Okoroji-Igbodike widely known as Wealthykate is the author of the Book: SMART THINKING, SMART IDEAS, WEALTHY PEOPLE. She is a retired Public Servant of the Federal Republic of Nigeria and a retired Foreign Service Officer. She worked and retired from the Nigerian Ministry of Foreign Affairs after the mandatory 35 years of service. She served as a Foreign Service Officer (diplomat) in many Nigerian Missions abroad, including New York in The United States of America and London in The United Kingdom. She holds a Masters Degree in Diplomatic Studies from the Diplomatic Academy of Westminster University, UK London.
